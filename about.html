Our Mission

To provide high quality, individualized lawn care programs designed to meet specific needs at affordable rates, as we educate our clients and help create and maintain healthy lawns.

About Us

Advanced Turf & Lawn Care, LLC has over 20 years of unmatched experience in turf management covering a wide range of terrains. The first 15 years involved large scale operations for numerous golf courses including PGA level courses. Our management history provides the experience and drive to develop a realistic plan, followed up with precise execution. We are experienced and licensed to apply the necessary chemicals to make your lawn healthy and green, while keeping children and pets safe. The past 5 years we have worked in private, residential lawn care management of a large property requiring numerous challenges. We are licensed and insured, so you can rest assured that you have professionals on the job.
